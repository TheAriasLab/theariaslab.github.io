Present members from Colombia

Undergraduate Students

Sebastian Reyes. Programa de Biologia - Universidad de Caldas. Sebastian Reyes - Phylogenomics and biogeography of Lepanthes (Pleurothallidinae: Orquidaceae): a model genus to understand the evolution of Andean orchids. He main goal of the proposed research is to reconstruct the evolutionary history of Lepanthes to use it as a framework for a new taxonomical classification. Additionally, we want to understand biogeographic patterns that could explain their high diversification and endemism in the Colombian Andes.
